Lot49

Lot49 is a lightning-network protocol developed by Global Mesh Labs which incentivizes the usage of Bitcoin for off-grid communication via mesh networks

In simple terms: If Alice wants to communicate to Bob, she can communicate to Alex, who is between her and Bob, who will take a routing fee in Bitcoin via Lightning to forward her message to Bob.

Global Mesh Labs has developed GoTenna, a mesh network device.
